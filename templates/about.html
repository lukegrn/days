About

Days is a photo project that will begin in 2026 and continue for the full year in which I take at least one picture every day, and upload one to this site. The purpose of this is to:

  1. Encourage me to shoot more.
  2. Document daily life in its mundanity as well as its exceptionality.
  3. Push me to carry my camera more places.
  4. Learn about how my style changes and stays the same over time.
  5. Learn what of my gear I use the most, and what I don't use often and could part with.

Rules:

  • No new gear
    • Camera: Sony a7iv
    • Lenses: Canon nFD 28mm f2.8 (Canon-FD mount), Zeiss Loxia 35mm f2 (Native Sony-E mount)*, Voigtlander 50mm f1.5 Nokton ii (Leica M-Mount)
    • iPhone 17 Pro - for the times where I can't/don't have my camera, RAWs make this much more usable. Likely will use the Halide app to turn off computational photography.
  • Can take as many pictures as I want each day, but only upload one. I must upload a photo taken that day, so if it is late and I don't have one something must be found.
  • Photos can be uploaded late, but must have been taken the day they are uploaded for. There are times I may not be able to import, edit, and upload a photo same-day.
  • Full color, I think. A part of this is to capture how this year felt, and I experienced it in color. If some B&W sneak in, that's okay, but as of now I imagine just full color.

*This lens's aperture ring turns the opposite direction of the other two, so there's a chance I will trade it in for a different 35 that changes aperture in the same direction as the other two, if it proves to be too annoying. If I do replace it, it will likely be with the Voigtlander 35mm 1.7 + plano convex 5.0m. Though, I will try very hard to not buy this if I don't have to.
