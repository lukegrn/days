About

"How we spend our days is, of course, how we spend our lives." - Annie Dillard

Days is a photo project that will begin in 2026 and continue for the full year in which I take at least one picture every day, and upload one to this site. The purpose of this is to:

  1. Encourage me to shoot more.
  2. Document daily life in its mundanity as well as its exceptionality.
  3. Push me to carry my camera more places.
  4. Learn about how my style changes and stays the same over time.
  5. Learn what of my gear I use the most, and what I don't use often and could part with.

Rules:

  • No new gear.
  • A mixture of film and digital.
  • Can take as many pictures as I want each day, but only upload one. I must upload a photo taken that day, so if it is late and I don't have one something must be found.
  • Photos can be uploaded late, but must have been taken the day they are uploaded for. There are times I may not be able to import, edit, and upload a photo same-day. In the case of film, it won't be uploaded until I can develop and scan the roll.
  • Full color, I think. A part of this is to capture how this year felt, and I experienced it in color. If some B&W sneak in, that's okay, but as of now I imagine just full color.
